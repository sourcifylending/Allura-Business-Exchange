## RESPONSE HANDLING

**If Buyer Signs NDA Quickly (Within 3-5 days):**
- Send thank you email immediately
- Attach full Presentation slide deck
- Schedule call for presentation and questions (within 1 week)
- Provide Tier 1 data room access (brand, marketing, platform overview materials)

**If Buyer Has NDA Questions (Legal/Counsel Review):**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Offer to discuss specific clauses
- Provide explanation or minor modifications if reasonable
- Keep modifications minimal; NDA is final unless major issue raised
- Do not agree to remove non-circumvention or confidentiality core clauses

**If Buyer Goes Quiet (No Response in 7-10 Days):**
- Send one follow-up email: "Checking in on your side – any questions I can answer?"
- If no response after 3 days, consider the opportunity on hold unless buyer re-engages
- Do not be pushy; qualify buyer's seriousness early

**If Buyer Rejects Terms:**
- Ask specifically what terms they want to modify
- Non-negotiables: Confidentiality, non-circumvention, no ownership transfer without payment
- Negotiable: Term length (3 years standard, could be 2-5 years), remedy language, venue (will work with buyer on Florida requirement)
- If buyer is serious, schedule call to discuss; email negotiation is slower
