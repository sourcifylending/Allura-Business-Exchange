# Workflows

## Idea To Sale
1. Capture idea in Market Radar.
2. Evaluate niche, demand, speed, and saleability.
3. Approve or reject the idea.
4. Create an asset draft.
5. Build the asset.
6. Package the asset.
7. Add listing data.
8. Publish the opportunity.
9. Capture buyer inquiry.
10. Qualify the buyer.
11. Send demo and summary.
12. Book the call.
13. Receive offer.
14. Send contract.
15. Confirm signature.
16. Confirm payment.
17. Unlock transfer.
18. Complete handoff.

## AI Asset Close Flow
1. Buyer clicks buy, make offer, or request to proceed.
2. Portal creates a deal record.
3. Portal generates a contract from deal data.
4. Portal sends contract for signature.
5. After all required signatures, portal creates a payment request record.
6. Payment status updates to paid after confirmed receipt.
7. Portal unlocks Transfer Desk.
8. Transfer checklist is completed.
9. Deal is marked closed.

## Buyer Workflow
1. Browse public or sanitized listings.
2. Save opportunities.
3. Submit buyer profile.
4. Request deeper access if needed.
5. Inquire on a listing.
6. Qualify for access or demo.
7. Review deal materials.
8. Negotiate and close.

## Business Opportunity Workflow
1. Review sanitized teaser.
2. Require approval or NDA for deeper access.
3. Underwrite opportunity.
4. Package deal materials.
5. Run offer and closing workflow.
6. Transfer documents and assets.

## Asset Factory Workflow
1. Identify niche and problem.
2. Validate demand.
3. Select starter template.
4. Create local project folder.
5. Build MVP.
6. Add branding and packaging.
7. Prepare demo and transfer materials.
8. List when ready.

## Transfer Workflow
Track:
- repo transfer
- domain transfer
- hosting transfer
- admin/account transfer
- file delivery
- documentation delivery
- support window
- final handoff complete

## Safety Rules
- Do not assume any external service connection.
- Do not automate outreach that behaves like spam.
- Do not make payment, e-sign, or deployment assumptions in Phase 0.
