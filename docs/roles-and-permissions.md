# Roles And Permissions

## Roles
- Super Admin
- Allura Operator
- Analyst
- Builder
- Sales Associate
- Buyer
- Seller
- Approved Buyer
- Approved Seller

## Permission Model
### Public Visitor
- view public pages
- view public AI asset listings
- view sanitized business teasers
- contact Allura

### Free Account User
- save opportunities
- submit buyer profile
- request deeper access
- view free account catalog views

### Approved Buyer
- access gated asset or deal-room materials where granted
- view deeper listing details for approved opportunities

### Seller / Submitter
- submit an opportunity or asset intake
- upload supporting materials where allowed

### Allura Operator
- control intake
- approve or reject ideas
- manage listings
- manage packaging
- manage inquiries
- manage offers
- manage transfers

### Super Admin
- full access across the platform
- manage settings and future integrations

## Boundaries
- Buyer access is approval-based for deeper materials.
- Business identities stay hidden by default.
- CRM and dialer remain placeholders in Phase 0.
